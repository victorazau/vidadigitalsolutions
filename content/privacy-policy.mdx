# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: April 5, 2025**

*Política de Privacidade — Política de Privacidad*

---

Vida Digital Solutions LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit vidadigitalsolutions.com, use Quasar CRM (quasarcrm.com), or engage our professional services.

We comply with the **GDPR** (EU/EEA), **LGPD** — Lei 13.709/2018 (Brazil), and **CCPA** (California).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us, schedule a call, sign a contract, or subscribe to Quasar CRM, we may collect: full name, company name, email address, phone number, business address, tax IDs (EIN, CNPJ, CPF), payment information, and project-related business data.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our websites: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site, referring URLs, and device identifiers — collected via cookies and analytics tools.

### 1.3 Information from Third-Party Platforms

In delivering our services, we may access data from platforms you authorize: GoHighLevel, ClickUp, Stripe, Asaas, ZapSign, Google Workspace, Meta Ads, and others. Access is limited to what is necessary for service delivery.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide, manage, and deliver our professional services and Quasar CRM
- To process payments via Stripe, Asaas, and other payment processors
- To communicate regarding projects, support, and account status (including via WhatsApp, email, SMS)
- To fulfill contractual obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
- To comply with legal and regulatory requirements in the US, Brazil, and EU
- To analyze and improve our services and website performance
- To send marketing communications (with consent where required)
- To prevent fraud and unauthorized access

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR & LGPD)

**Contract performance**: Service delivery, billing, project management.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

### GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

- **Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure** — Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction** — Limit how we process your data
- **Portability** — Receive your data in a portable format
- **Object** — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent** — At any time, where processing is consent-based

### LGPD Rights (Brazilian Users)

Confirmation of processing, access, correction, anonymization/blocking/deletion of unnecessary data, portability, information about third-party sharing, review of automated decisions, and revocation of consent.

### CCPA Rights (California Residents)

Right to know, right to delete, right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell data), and right to non-discrimination.

**To exercise any right**, contact us at privacy@vidadigitalsolutions.com. We respond within 30 days.

## 9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmitted through our websites and APIs
- Access controls on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security assessments
- Multi-factor authentication for internal systems

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you and relevant supervisory authorities as required by law (within 72 hours under GDPR).
