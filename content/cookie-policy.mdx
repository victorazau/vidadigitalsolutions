Vida Digital Solutions LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our websites: **vidadigitalsolutions.com** and **quasarcrm.com**.

This policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use, and how you can control them. It complements our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your preferences, analyze usage patterns, and deliver relevant content.

**Similar technologies we use:**
- **Local storage** — browser-based data storage for remembering your preferences
- **Session storage** — temporary storage cleared when you close your browser
- **Pixels / tracking scripts** — code embedded by third parties (e.g., Meta Pixel, Google Tag)

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They cannot be disabled.

- `vds_cookie_consent` — Stores your cookie consent preferences (1 year)
- `vds_lang` — Remembers your language selection EN/PT/ES (1 year)
- `__cf_bm` — Cloudflare bot protection (30 minutes)
- `cf_clearance` — Cloudflare security challenge (1 year)

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. They collect anonymous, aggregated data.

- `_ga` — Google Analytics, distinguish users (2 years)
- `_ga_*` — Google Analytics, session tracking (2 years)
- `_gid` — Google Analytics, distinguish users 24h (24 hours)
- `_gat` — Google Analytics, throttle request rate (1 minute)

We use Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled. Data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

### 2.3 Marketing & Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and display relevant ads.

- `_fbp` — Meta (Facebook/Instagram), ad targeting and conversion tracking (3 months)
- `_fbc` — Meta, click ID for ad attribution (2 years)
- `ttclid` — TikTok, ad attribution (30 days)
- `li_sugr` — LinkedIn, ad targeting (3 months)

### 2.4 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

- `vds_theme` — Stores your dark/light mode preference (1 year)
- `intercom-*` — Live chat functionality if active (Session)

## 4. Your Cookie Choices

### 4.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

- **Accept all cookies** — enables all categories
- **Reject non-essential cookies** — only essential cookies are used
- **Manage preferences** — choose which categories to enable

Your choice is saved in the `vds_cookie_consent` cookie for 1 year. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the **"Cookie Settings"** link in the footer.

### 4.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox**: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

### 4.3 Opt-Out Links

- Google Analytics: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- Google Ads: adssettings.google.com
- Meta Ads: facebook.com/adpreferences
- LinkedIn Ads: linkedin.com/psettings/guest-controls
- Your Online Choices (EU): youronlinechoices.eu
