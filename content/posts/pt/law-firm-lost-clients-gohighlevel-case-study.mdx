## The Hidden Cost of Manual Lead Management

Miguel Santos runs a successful immigration law practice in Miami, specializing in helping Brazilian families navigate the US immigration system. His firm was generating 40-50 leads monthly through referrals, Google Ads, and community events. However, their lead management process was entirely manual:

- Leads filled out a contact form on the website
- Paralegal manually entered information into Excel
- Attorney called prospects when available (often 3-5 days later)
- No systematic follow-up for missed calls
- No nurturing sequence for prospects not ready to hire immediately

The results were devastating. Of 45 leads per month:
- 18 prospects (40%) never received any follow-up
- 12 prospects (27%) were contacted too late and hired competitors
- Only 15 prospects (33%) actually scheduled consultations
- Of those, only 8-10 showed up for appointments

"We were working harder than ever but our conversion rates were terrible," Miguel explains. "I knew we had a problem when prospects started telling us they hired other attorneys because we took too long to respond."
