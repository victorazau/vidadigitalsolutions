## What Is Move-in / Move-out Cleaning (and Why It Pays More)

Move-in cleaning is the deep cleaning performed on a property **before new residents move in**. Move-out cleaning is the service done **after the previous tenants have left** — typically to recover a security deposit or prepare the unit for the next renter.

These services require more time, more supplies, and more attention to detail than a standard weekly clean. That's why the average ticket is significantly higher. A two-bedroom unit can bring in between $350 and $500, while a maintenance clean on that same property might go for $120 to $160.

Beyond the price, these clients have **real urgency**: they need the cleaning done by a specific date tied to their lease agreement, landlord inspection, or move-in day. Whoever responds first wins the contract.

## Why Summer Is the Golden Window for These Contracts

Summer in the US concentrates the highest residential turnover of the year for three key reasons:

1. **End of college leases** — students vacate apartments in May/June and move into new ones in August
2. **Airbnb and short-term rental turnover** — property owners need deep cleans between guests, especially heading into peak summer bookings
3. **Families moving between school years** — July is the preferred month to relocate without pulling kids out of school mid-year

The practical result: in July and August, demand for move-in/move-out cleaning can run **up to 40% higher** than the rest of the year. ## How to Price Move-in / Move-out Cleaning Correctly

Common mistake: pricing it like a standard clean. Move-in/move-out is deep cleaning — bathrooms scrubbed top to bottom, inside cabinets, inside the oven, inside the fridge, windows, baseboards, closets. The time required can be **2 to 3 times longer** than a maintenance clean.

Market reference pricing for 2026 in the US:

| Property Size | Price Range (move-out) |
|---|---|
| Studio / 1 bedroom | $250 – $350 |
| 2 bedrooms / 1 bathroom | $350 – $450 |
| 3 bedrooms / 2 bathrooms | $450 – $600 |
| 4+ bedrooms | $600 – $900+ |
| Airbnb / short-term rental | $150 – $300 (per turnover) |

Add charges for extras like carpet cleaning, exterior window washing, or mold remediation. These add-ons can increase your ticket by 20–40%.

**Important tip:** publish your pricing (or at least a price range) on your website. Clients searching for move-out cleaning want transparency — and both Google and AI tools prioritize pages that directly answer pricing questions.
