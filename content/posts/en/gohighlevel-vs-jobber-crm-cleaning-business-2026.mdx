## What Is Jobber and Who Is It Built For?

**Jobber** is an American field service management software built specifically for service businesses like cleaning, landscaping, HVAC, and pest control. It was created to solve one very specific problem: organizing your day-to-day operations — who goes where, when, and how much to charge.

With Jobber, you can:

- Create and send professional estimates in minutes
- Schedule jobs and view your team's calendar
- Send automatic appointment reminders to clients
- Track team check-in/check-out in the field
- Send invoices and accept credit card payments
- Give your employees a simple mobile app to use on the job

It's a solid, well-reviewed tool with over 200,000 users. But it has a very clear focus: **operations**, not growth.

## When Does Jobber Make More Sense?

Jobber has the edge in a few specific situations:

**1. You have a field team and need route management**
If you have 5 or more cleaners working different areas at the same time, Jobber's app for managing check-ins, routes, and service time is more mature than what GoHighLevel currently offers.

**2. You need detailed invoicing and billing**
For sending professional invoices, accepting recurring credit card payments, and accessing simple financial reports, Jobber is more fully featured out of the box.

**3. You're not focused on digital marketing right now**
If your schedule is already full, referrals are working great, and you simply need to organize your operations, Jobber handles it well — and it's easier to get started with.
