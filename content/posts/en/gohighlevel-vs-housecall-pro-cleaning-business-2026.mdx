## What Each Tool Does (And Who It's For)

Before comparing feature by feature, it's important to understand what each platform is actually designed to do.

**Housecall Pro** is field service management software — built for businesses that send technicians or crews to client locations. Think of it as a smart dispatch system. It answers questions like: *Who's going where tomorrow? Did the client pay? Did the crew confirm their arrival time?*

**GoHighLevel** is a CRM and marketing automation platform. It's built to capture leads, nurture prospects, automate communication, and convert contacts into paying clients. It answers questions like: *How do I get more people to find my business? How do I follow up with someone who requested a quote but never responded? How do I automatically ask for Google reviews after every job?*

Simply put: one is an operations tool, the other is a growth tool.

## When Housecall Pro Is the Right Choice

Honesty matters here: Housecall Pro is the better option in certain specific scenarios.

Choose Housecall Pro if:
