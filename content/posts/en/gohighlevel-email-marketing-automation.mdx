## Setting Up Your First Email Campaign

The first step to automate emails in GoHighLevel is properly configuring your base campaign.

### Initial Preparation

Before creating any automation, make sure you have:
- Authenticated domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Email templates created and tested
- Tags and custom fields defined
- Audience segments mapped

### Creating the Campaign

1. **Access Campaigns > Email:** navigate to the campaigns section
2. **Create New Campaign:** select "Email Marketing" type
3. **Configure Audience:** define segments using tags or filters
4. **Design Template:** use the visual editor or import HTML templates
5. **Set Automation Rules:** configure triggers and conditions

**Important tip:** always test your campaign with a small list before mass sending. Vida Digital Solutions recommends using groups of 50-100 contacts for initial A/B testing.

## Creating Effective Nurture Sequences

Nurture sequences are the heart of email marketing automation. In GoHighLevel, you can create complex flows that respond to user behavior in real-time.

### Structure of an Effective Sequence

**Email 1 - Welcome (Immediate send):**
- Company introduction
- Setting expectations
- First value delivery (ebook, checklist, etc.)

**Email 2 - Educational (3 days later):**
- Relevant educational content
- Success case or testimonial
- Soft CTA for engagement

**Email 3 - Social Proof (1 week):**
- Detailed success stories
- Metrics and results
- Authority building

**Email 4 - Offer (10 days):**
- Solution presentation
- Clear benefits
- Conversion CTA

### Configuration in Workflow Builder

GoHighLevel's Workflow Builder allows you to create these sequences visually:

1. **Initial trigger:** when lead enters the list
2. **Wait actions:** intervals between emails
3. **Conditional logic:** branches based on behavior
4. **Action steps:** email sending, tag application, pipeline changes

To maximize results, configure conditions that move leads to different paths based on:
- Open rates of previous emails
- Clicks on specific links
- Website page visits
- Social media interactions

## Sales Pipeline Integration

One of GoHighLevel's biggest advantages is the native integration between email marketing and sales pipeline. This allows you to create automations that respond dynamically to lead progress in the funnel.

### Pipeline-based Automations

**Lead enters pipeline:**
- Automatic welcome email sending
- Specific tag application
- Nurture sequence initiation

**Lead advances stage:**
- Current sequence pause
- New stage-appropriate sequence start
- Sales team notification

**Lead stalls in a stage:**
- Reactivation sequence activation
- Manual follow-up scheduling
- "attention needed" tag application

**Lead converts:**
- All sales sequences pause
- Onboarding sequence start
- Transfer to post-sale flows

### Practical Configuration

To set up these automations:

1. **Access Automation > Workflows**
2. **Create triggers based on:** "Contact Pipeline Changed"
3. **Configure conditions:** specific stage, time in stage, deal value
4. **Define actions:** email sending, tag changes, notifications

This integration ensures your email campaigns are always relevant to the exact moment in the lead's buying journey.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the best frequency for automated email sending?
Ideal frequency varies by segment, but generally 1-2 emails per week works well for nurturing. For welcome sequences, you can be more aggressive in the first days (1 email/day) then space out. Always monitor unsubscribe rates to adjust.

### How do I improve email deliverability in GoHighLevel?
Maintain clean lists, authenticate your domain correctly (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), avoid spam trigger words, and warm up new domains gradually. Also monitor engagement metrics - low engagement emails hurt your reputation.

### Can I integrate GoHighLevel email marketing with other tools?
Yes, GoHighLevel offers integrations via Zapier and webhooks with hundreds of tools. You can connect with Google Analytics, Facebook Ads, ERP systems, and much more to create even more powerful automations.

### How long does it take to see results with email automation?
Initial results appear in 2-4 weeks, but true impact manifests after 2-3 months of constant optimizations. It's important to be patient and focus on lead quality and content before expecting large conversion volumes.
