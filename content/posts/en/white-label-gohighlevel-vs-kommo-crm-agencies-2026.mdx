White-label GoHighLevel and Kommo are two popular solutions for agencies looking to resell CRM as their own product. **GoHighLevel offers complete SaaS Mode with advanced automation features and full white-labeling for $497/month, while Kommo focuses on B2B sales with messaging platforms starting at $15/user/month.** The choice depends on your business model: GoHighLevel is ideal for agencies wanting to build recurring revenue by reselling complete CRM solutions, while Kommo is better for internal sales teams needing WhatsApp and Telegram integration.

With the growing automation market in 2026, many agencies are seeking alternatives to expand revenue by offering proprietary CRM to clients. The decision between these two platforms can define the financial future of your operation.

## What Is White-Label and Why It Matters for Agencies

White-label allows agencies to resell software as if it were their own product. In the CRM context, this means applying your brand, setting prices, and creating monthly recurring revenue without developing technology from scratch.

GoHighLevel revolutionized this market with SaaS Mode, allowing agencies to create complete software versions with custom domains, personalized colors, and even mobile apps with their branding.

Kommo (formerly amoCRM) offers a more traditional white-label partnership, focused on resellers serving specific markets, especially companies selling through messaging platforms like WhatsApp.
