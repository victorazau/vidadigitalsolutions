## 1. Lead Capture and Nurturing Workflow (Lead Magnet Automation)

**The Problem:** Most businesses capture the lead and... stop there. The potential customer downloads an ebook, subscribes to a list, but receives no structured follow-up. Result: 90% of leads are wasted.

**The Solution:** This workflow starts the moment someone downloads your lead magnet and creates a 7-day nurturing journey that transforms visitors into paying customers.

**How It Works:**
- **Trigger:** Lead downloads free material
- **Immediate Action:** Delivery email + welcome SMS
- **Day 1:** Relevant success story via email
- **Day 3:** SMS asking about biggest challenge
- **Day 5:** Personalized video explaining solution
- **Day 7:** Special time-limited discount offer

**Real Results:** A marketing consultancy we implemented this workflow for saw:
- **43% increase** in lead-to-customer conversion rate
- **267% more bookings** for free consultations
- **18 hours per week** saved on manual follow-up

## 2. Inactive Customer Reactivation Workflow (Win-Back Campaign)

**The Problem:** You've had hundreds or thousands of customers, but many stopped buying months ago. This list of inactive customers is an untapped gold mine.

**The Solution:** An intelligent workflow that identifies customers who haven't purchased in X days and creates a personalized multichannel reactivation campaign.

**Strategic Setup:**

- **Channel 1:** "We miss you" email with special offer

- **Channel 3:** Automated call if no SMS response
- **Channel 4:** Facebook/Google Ads retargeting

**Success Case:** A supplement e-commerce that **Vida Digital Solutions** automated recovered:
- **$47,000 in sales** in the first 30 days
- **28% reactivation rate** for customers inactive 6+ months

## 10. Canceled Customer Recovery Workflow

**The Problem:** Customer cancels and you passively accept it. Don't try to understand the real reason or offer alternatives. Lose the customer forever.

**The Solution:** System that intervenes in the cancellation process, tries to solve the problem, and if unsuccessful, keeps the door open for future return.

## Fatal Implementation Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Implementing All Workflows at Once
This is the most common mistake. Companies get excited and try to automate everything simultaneously. Result: nothing works well.

**Solution:** Implement 1-2 workflows at a time, measure results, optimize, and only then move to the next.

### 2. Not Personalizing Communication
Using generic templates in all workflows kills effectiveness. Customers notice robotic messages.

**Solution:** Use dynamic fields, behavioral segmentation, and consistent tone of voice with your brand.

### 3. Ignoring Mobile
Over 70% of emails are opened on mobile, but many workflows are created thinking only about desktop.

**Solution:** Test all workflows on mobile devices and optimize the experience.
