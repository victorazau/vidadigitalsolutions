## What Is Follow-up Automation (and Why It Changes Everything)

Follow-up automation is a system that sends automatic messages — via SMS, email, or WhatsApp — to clients and leads at the right moments, without you having to do anything manually.

Here's how it works in practice:

1. **New lead comes in through your website or Google** → receives an automatic response in under 2 minutes with an estimated price and a link to book.
2. **Service is completed** → the next day, the client gets a thank-you SMS asking for a Google review.
3. **21 days later** → the system automatically sends a message asking if they'd like to schedule their next cleaning.
4. **Client doesn't respond** → 7 days later, a second gentle follow-up. Then a third with a special offer for recurring service.
5. **Client books recurring service** → enters an automatic reminder sequence before each upcoming appointment.

All of this happens while you're cleaning a home, picking up your kids from school, or sleeping.
