## GoHighLevel vs Kommo: Platform Overview

**GoHighLevel** is an all-in-one platform created specifically for marketing agencies and service businesses. It combines CRM, marketing automation, funnel builder, appointment scheduling, email marketing, SMS, and much more in a single tool. Vida Digital Solutions has observed exponential growth in GoHighLevel adoption among American and international businesses.

**Kommo** (formerly amoCRM) is a conversational CRM focused on simplicity and customer communication. It was born with a focus on direct sales and positions itself as a more affordable and easy-to-implement solution.

### Key Structural Differences

- **GoHighLevel**: Complete ecosystem with 20+ integrated tools
- **Kommo**: Focused CRM with external integrations for extra functionality
- **Target audience**: GHL for agencies and larger businesses; Kommo for SMBs
- **Complexity**: GHL more robust; Kommo simpler

## Feature Comparison: GoHighLevel vs Kommo

### Contact Management and Pipeline

**GoHighLevel** offers a highly customizable pipeline system with native automation. You can create multiple pipelines by service type, automate lead movement based on actions, and integrate directly with marketing campaigns.

**Kommo** provides a simple and efficient visual pipeline, but with fewer customization options. The interface is cleaner and more intuitive for teams that don't need excessive complexity.

### Automation and Marketing

This is where GoHighLevel really shines. The platform offers visual workflow builder, multichannel automation (email, SMS, WhatsApp, voice calls), and AI features for automated customer service.

Kommo has basic automations, focused primarily on sales follow-up and internal notifications. For advanced marketing, you'll need to integrate external tools.
