**GoHighLevel's Workflow AI** is a revolutionary feature that allows you to create complex automations simply by describing what you want in natural language. Instead of manually building flows, you simply explain your objective and artificial intelligence automatically assembles the entire workflow, including triggers, conditions, and actions.

## 1. What is GoHighLevel's Workflow AI

Workflow AI is an intelligent assistant integrated into GoHighLevel that transforms plain text descriptions into complete functional automations. Launched in 2025, this tool represents a milestone in democratizing business automation.

### How It Works in Practice

When you access the Workflows section in GoHighLevel, you'll find the **"Create with AI"** option next to the traditional creation button. When clicked, a chat interface opens where you can describe exactly what you need.

For example, instead of manually configuring a lead follow-up workflow, you simply write: *"I want to automate follow-up for leads who downloaded my e-book. Send 3 emails over 7 days and then transfer to a sales funnel."*

The AI processes this request and automatically creates:
- Trigger based on e-book download
- Sequence of 3 emails with appropriate intervals
- Conditions to check opens and clicks
- Final action to change pipeline

### Workflow AI Advantages

**Implementation Speed**: What used to take 30-60 minutes to configure manually is now created in 2-3 minutes.

**Fewer Errors**: AI automatically considers scenarios that inexperienced users frequently forget, such as bounce handling and exit conditions.

**Built-in Best Practices**: The system already includes optimized timing, intelligent segmentation, and tested structures.

## 2. How to Access and Configure Workflow AI

To use Workflow AI, you need to be on a GoHighLevel Agency or SaaS account. This functionality is not available on the Starter plan.

### Step-by-Step Access Guide

**Step 1**: In the left sidebar menu of GoHighLevel, click **"Marketing"** then **"Workflows"**.

**Step 2**: On the workflows screen, you'll see two buttons in the upper right corner: **"Create Workflow"** (traditional) and **"Create with AI"** (new).

**Step 3**: Click **"Create with AI"**. A modal window opens with a large text field and a **"Generate Workflow"** button.

**Step 4**: Type your request in English. Be specific about:
- What the trigger is (what starts the automation)
- What actions you want to execute
- What the final objective is
- Desired timing between actions

### Important Initial Settings

Before creating your first workflow with AI, make sure your GoHighLevel account has:

- **Email configured**: SMTP or integration with email provider
- **Pipelines created**: AI needs to know where to move leads
- **Tags organized**: For proper segmentation
- **Calendar configured**: If the workflow includes scheduling

## 3. Creating Your First AI Workflow

Let's create a real workflow using Workflow AI. The example will be a welcome automation for new leads who signed up on the website.

### Practical Example: Welcome Workflow

**Step 1**: In the Workflow AI interface, type:

*"Create welcome workflow for new leads. When someone fills out the website form, I want to: send welcome email immediately, after 2 days send email with success case study, after 5 days make automatic call, and after 7 days send commercial proposal. If person responds to any email or answers phone call, move to 'Interested' pipeline."*

**Step 2**: Click **"Generate Workflow"**. AI will process for 10-15 seconds.

**Step 3**: The system will automatically create:
- **Trigger**: "Form Submitted" on specified form
- **Action 1**: Send Email (welcome template)
- **Wait**: 2 days
- **Action 2**: Send Email (success case study)
- **Wait**: 3 days
- **Action 3**: Create Task (call the lead)
- **Wait**: 2 days
- **Action 4**: Send Email (commercial proposal)
- **Conditions**: If/Then for pipeline change based on engagement

### Refining the Created Workflow

After automatic creation, you can:

**Edit Templates**: Click on each email action to customize content

**Adjust Timing**: Modify intervals between actions if needed

**Add Conditions**: Include additional filters like business hours or location

**Configure Notifications**: Set alerts for your team about engaged leads

## 4. Advanced Prompts for Complex Workflows

To get the most out of Workflow AI, you need to master the art of writing effective prompts. Here are advanced techniques that Vida Digital Solutions uses with clients.

### Ideal Prompt Structure

An effective prompt should contain:

1. **Context**: Type of business and objective
2. **Specific trigger**: What starts the automation
3. **Action sequence**: What to do in chronological order
4. **Conditions**: When to change course
5. **Final objective**: Where you want to end up

### Complex Prompt Example for Law Firms

*"I'm a law firm that handles immigration cases. I want to automate free consultation follow-up. When someone schedules consultation through calendar, I need: 1 day before consultation send SMS with reminder and Zoom link, 2 hours before send email with document checklist, if person attends consultation tag 'Attended' and send proposal by email next day, if they don't attend automatically reschedule for new date and send sequence of 3 emails explaining importance of immigration regularization. If person accepts proposal, move to 'Active Clients' pipeline."*

This prompt will result in a sophisticated workflow with multiple branches and conditions.

### Prompts by Market Segment

**For Real Estate**:
*"Automate nurturing for leads interested in buying homes. Trigger: 'Want to Buy' form submission. Send series of 5 emails over 2 weeks with financing tips, trending neighborhoods, and satisfied client case studies. Every 3 days attempt phone contact. If lead responds or answers, automatically schedule showing. If no engagement after 14 days, move to long-term nurturing list."*

**For Medical Clinics**:
*"Workflow to reduce appointment no-shows. Trigger: appointment scheduled. 7 days before send educational email about procedure, 3 days before SMS with confirmation, 1 day before automatic confirmation call, 2 hours before send location via WhatsApp. If patient confirms at any point, stop reminders. If they don't show, automatically reschedule and start reactivation sequence."*

## 5. Optimizing Performance of AI-Created Workflows

An AI-created workflow is just the starting point. To get exceptional results, you need to continuously monitor and optimize.

### Essential Metrics to Track

**Email Open Rate**: Ideal above 25%. If low, test new subject lines.

**Click Rate**: Target 3-5%. Optimize call-to-actions and content if needed.

**Final Conversion**: Percentage of leads reaching workflow objective.

**Average Time to Conversion**: Identify bottlenecks in the flow.

### A/B Testing in AI Workflows

GoHighLevel allows creating different versions of the same workflow to test:

- **Different timing**: Test 2 vs 3 day intervals between emails
- **Alternative content**: Direct emails vs more educational
- **Different channels**: SMS vs Email vs WhatsApp
- **Modified sequence**: Different order of actions

## 6. Advanced Use Cases and Troubleshooting

### Multi-Channel Workflows with AI

One of the great advantages of Workflow AI is automatically creating flows that intelligently use multiple communication channels.

**Multi-Channel Prompt Example**:
*"Create friendly collection workflow for overdue clients. Start with educational email on day 1 after due date, SMS on day 3, automatic call on day 7, WhatsApp on day 10, firmer email on day 15, and finally transfer to external collections on day 30. If client pays at any time, stop workflow and send thank you."*

### Common Problem Solutions

**Problem**: AI-created workflow not triggering
**Solution**: Verify trigger is correctly configured and leads are actually performing expected action.

**Problem**: Emails going to spam
**Solution**: Configure SPF, DKIM and DMARC properly. The [email marketing automation](/blog/gohighlevel-email-marketing-automation) has specific deliverability settings.

**Problem**: Workflow too complex and confusing
**Solution**: Break into smaller, more specific workflows. Use tags to connect them.

### Advanced Post-Creation Customization

After AI creates the base workflow, you can add:

- **Time Conditions**: Only send emails during business hours
- **Geographic Filters**: Different actions by location
- **Behavioral Segmentation**: Special treatment for more engaged leads
- **Automatic Escalation**: Notify managers when leads don't convert
