## Step 1 — Set Up Your Calendar in GoHighLevel

**What you'll see on screen:** After logging into GoHighLevel (or Quasar CRM, the white-label solution from Vida Digital Solutions), go to the left sidebar and click **"Calendars"**. In the top right corner, click **"Create Calendar"**.

You'll see three calendar type options:
- **Round Robin** — distributes bookings among multiple team members (ideal if you have a crew)
- **Event Calendar** — for events with multiple available spots
- **Personal / Simple** — perfect to start with, a single unified schedule

**For cleaning companies just getting started with automation, choose "Simple Calendar."**

### Essential calendar settings:

1. **Calendar name:** Use something clear like "Book Your Cleaning" or "Schedule a Cleaning"
2. **Appointment duration:** Set 30 minutes (for a quote or consultation) or 2–4 hours (for a full cleaning job)
3. **Available hours:** Configure your actual working days and hours — don't let the system show Saturday night availability if you don't work then
4. **Buffer time:** Add 30–60 minutes between appointments for travel time
5. **Minimum scheduling notice:** Set at least 24 hours so you don't receive last-minute bookings you can't fulfill

**Pro tip:** If you clean in different neighborhoods on different days — for example, one area on Mondays and Tuesdays, another area on Wednesdays and Thursdays — create a separate calendar for each service zone. This eliminates unnecessary drive time and protects your profit margin.

## Step 2 — Build Your Pre-Booking Form

**What you'll see on screen:** Still inside "Calendars", go to the **"Forms"** tab within your calendar, or navigate to **"Sites > Forms"** in the left sidebar.

The pre-booking form is what clients fill out before confirming their time slot. For a cleaning business, collect the following information:

| Field | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Full name | Personalizes follow-up messages |
| Phone number (SMS) | Confirmations and reminders |
| Email address | Formal communication and email sequences |
| Property address | Plan routing and crew assignments |
| Type of cleaning | Standard, deep clean, move-out, Airbnb turnover |
| Property size (sq ft) | Calculate pricing and job duration |
| How did you find us? | Track your marketing sources |

**Make the phone number field required.** Without a cell number, your SMS automation won't work — and SMS has a 98% open rate compared to 20–30% for email.

### Connecting the form to your calendar:

In GoHighLevel, you can configure the form to appear **before** the time slot selection (client fills in their info first, then picks a slot) or **after** (they pick a time first). For cleaning companies, we recommend **info first** — that way you can qualify the lead before confirming availability.

## Step 3 — Set Up Automatic Confirmations

**What you'll see on screen:** Still inside your calendar settings, click on the **"Notifications"** or **"Confirmations"** tab.

This is where you configure the messages clients automatically receive after booking.

### Immediate email confirmation (sent at 0 minutes):

Sample text for a cleaning company:
> *"Hi [First Name]! Your cleaning appointment is confirmed for [Appointment Date] at [Appointment Time]. Our team will arrive at [Property Address]. Questions? Reply to this email or call us at [Your Number]. See you soon! — [Your Company Name]"*

### Immediate SMS confirmation (sent at 0 minutes):

> *"Hi [First Name]! ✅ Cleaning confirmed for [Date] at [Time]. Address: [Property Address]. Questions? Text or call [Your Number]."*

**Pro tip:** GoHighLevel lets you personalize these messages with dynamic variables like `{{contact.first_name}}`, `{{appointment.start_time}}`, and `{{appointment.location}}`. Always use them — personalized messages get 3x more engagement than generic ones.

## Step 4 — Build Your Reminder and Follow-Up Workflow

This is where the magic happens. A Workflow in GoHighLevel is an automated sequence of actions triggered by an event — in this case, a calendar booking.

**What you'll see on screen:** In the left sidebar, click **"Automation > Workflows"**, then click **"+ New Workflow"**. Choose **"Start from Scratch"**.

### Configure your Trigger:

Click **"Add Trigger"** and select **"Appointment"** → **"Appointment Status Changed"** → select **"Booked"**. This means: every time someone books an appointment, this workflow will run.

### Recommended action sequence for cleaning companies:

**Action 1 — Immediately:** Send SMS confirmation (already configured in Step 3, but you can reinforce it here with a different message)

**Action 2 — Wait until 24 hours before the appointment:** Use the "Wait" block with a time condition relative to the appointment

**Action 3 — 24-hour reminder (SMS):**
> *"Hi [First Name]! Quick reminder: your cleaning is tomorrow at [Time] at [Address]. Need to reschedule? Click here: [Reschedule Link]. See you tomorrow! 🧹"*

**Action 4 — Wait until 2 hours before the appointment:**

**Action 5 — 2-hour reminder (SMS):**
> *"Hi [First Name]! Our team is getting ready for your cleaning today at [Time]. We'll be at [Address]. See you soon!"*

**Action 6 — Wait 4 hours after the appointment end time:**

**Action 7 — Review request (SMS):**
> *"Hi [First Name]! Hope your home is sparkling clean! 🌟 If you're happy with our service, a quick Google review means the world to us: [Google Review Link]. Thank you! — [Company Name]"*

This sequence alone can increase your **Google review volume by 30–40%** — which directly impacts your ranking on Google Maps.

## Step 5 — Add Your Booking Link to Your Website and Profiles

**What you'll see on screen:** Back in **"Calendars"**, click on your calendar and go to **"Share"** or **"Embed"**. GoHighLevel will generate:

- A **direct link** to your calendar (e.g., `yourdomain.com/book`)
- An **embed code** to place the calendar directly on your website
- A **widget** that appears as a floating button on your site

### Where to place your booking link:

1. **Website:** "Book Now" button in the header, hero section, and at the bottom of every service page
2. **Google Business Profile:** Add the link under "Book an appointment" in your profile settings
3. **Instagram Bio:** Direct link to your calendar (or use a link-in-bio tool like Linktree)
4. **Facebook Business Page:** Set it as your "Book Now" action button
5. **Email signature:** Include the link in all business correspondence

**Pro tip:** Create a clean, memorable URL for your booking link. In GoHighLevel, you can set this up under "Sites > Funnels" or use a custom subdomain like `book.yourcleaningcompany.com`.

## Step 6 — Test Everything Before You Go Live

This step is skipped by 80% of people — and it's the most important one.

**What you'll see on screen:** Open your booking link in an **incognito browser window** (Ctrl+Shift+N in Chrome) — this way you see exactly what your clients see, without being logged into the system.

### Testing checklist:

- [ ] Does the form load correctly on mobile? (Over 70% of traffic comes from mobile devices)
- [ ] Are the required fields working properly?
- [ ] Do the available time slots show up correctly in the right time zone?
- [ ] After confirming, did you receive the SMS and email confirmation?
- [ ] Did the event appear in your Google Calendar (if you have it integrated)?
- [ ] Was the reminder sequence properly added to the workflow?
- [ ] Does the reschedule link work?

Make a real test booking using your own cell phone number. Wait for the reminders to come in. Only open it up to clients once everything works perfectly.
