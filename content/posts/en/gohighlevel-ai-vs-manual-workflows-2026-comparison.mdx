## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does Workflow AI completely replace manual construction?**

**A:** Not completely. Workflow AI is excellent for 80% of standard use cases, but highly specific workflows or those with complex logic still need manual construction. The trend is to use AI as a starting point and customize manually when necessary.

**Q: How long does it take to master each approach?**

**A:** Workflow AI can be mastered in 1-2 days of practice, as it depends more on knowing how to describe processes clearly. Manual workflows require 2-4 weeks of study and practice to master advanced features like conditional logic and API integrations.

**Q: Which performs better: AI-created or manual workflows?**

**A:** Performance depends more on strategy than construction method. Well-built manual workflows tend to be more efficient due to high optimization, but well-configured AI workflows come very close to manual performance with much less effort.

**Q: Can I migrate an AI workflow to manual later?**

**A:** Yes, you can use an AI workflow as a foundation and then customize manually. This is a very efficient strategy: use AI to get the skeleton ready and then manually refine the specific points of your business.
